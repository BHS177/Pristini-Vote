## Deploy to Vercel (Easiest - 5 minutes)

### Step 1: Go to Vercel
Visit: **https://vercel.com**

### Step 2: Sign Up/Login
- Sign up with GitHub (recommended) or email

### Step 3: Import Project
1. Click **"Add New Project"**
2. Click **"Import Git Repository"**
3. Select your **`Pristini-Vote`** repository
4. Click **"Import"**

### Step 4: Configure
- **Framework Preset:** Leave as "Other" or select "Node.js"
- **Root Directory:** `./` (default)
- **Build Command:** (leave empty)
- **Output Directory:** (leave empty)
- **Install Command:** `npm install`

### Step 5: Deploy
- Click **"Deploy"**
- Wait 2-3 minutes

### Step 6: Get Your URLs
After deployment, you'll get:
- **Main Voting URL:** `https://pristini-vote.vercel.app` (share with students)
- **Admin URL:** `https://pristini-vote.vercel.app/admin` (share ONLY with president)
